Rice/Lunch/35/2cups Rice, 3.5cups water, 1tbspn Olive Oil, 2tbsp Butter, 2tbsp Orzo, 1.5tsp Salt, /Wash the rice and keep it aside in a strainer to allow the water to drain.
Put the olive oil and butter in a pan. Fry the orzos until they turn golden brown.
Add the drained rice and start frying.
Add the salt.
After frying the rice on low heat for 3-4 minutes, stirring constantly, add 3.5 cups of boiling water into it.
Close the lid of the pot. Cook for 3-4 minutes on high heat, then for 3-4 minutes on medium heat, and finally over low heat until the grains are cooked and the water is absorbed.
When the water is absorbed, remove from the stove and leave to infuse for 20 minutes.
